# Private

The **Private** application is for automating your own financial operations. Unlike Whitelabel or OAuth, there is no external customer — the application acts on your own Monerium account.

#### Who is it for?

- **Corporate treasuries:** Automate payroll, vendor payments, or liquidity management.
- **Traders & developers:** Build personal trading bots, algorithms, or specialized financial tools.
- **Backend automation:** High-frequency or low-latency fund movements between SEPA and the blockchain.

#### Who is it NOT for?

- **Customer-facing platforms:** Use Whitelabel for managing external users.
- **Public DeFi protocols:** Use OAuth for community-driven apps.

## Webhooks

[API Reference →](https://docs.monerium.com/api/#tag/webhooks)

Webhooks let your backend react in real-time to payment events without polling.

### Setting up webhooks

- **Registration:** Register your target URL via the Webhooks endpoint using your Client Credentials token.
- **Verification:** Respond with `200 OK` to the `subscription.created` event to activate.
- **Secret key:** Store the webhook secret securely — required to verify incoming messages.

### Signature verification

Every request includes a `webhook-signature` header — an HMAC-SHA256 hash of the `webhook-id`, `webhook-timestamp`, and raw request body. Verify it with your secret before processing.

### Events

- **`order.created`:** Triggered when you receive a SEPA transfer and EURe is minted to your wallet.
- **`order.updated`:** Tracks the lifecycle of outgoing payments.

### Best practices

- Return `200 OK` immediately; process asynchronously to avoid timeouts.
- Monerium retries with exponential backoff up to 10 times over 12 hours.
- Use `webhook-id` to deduplicate retried events.

## IBAN

[API Reference →](https://docs.monerium.com/api/#tag/ibans)

Your IBAN is the gateway between SEPA and your blockchain wallet. For Private applications, this is typically a **one-time setup**.

- **Recommended:** Manage your IBAN directly in the [Monerium.app](https://monerium.app) dashboard — the simplest path for personal use.
- **Programmatic provisioning:** Available via the API, but the UI-based setup is generally preferred when not managing multiple users.
- Incoming funds are minted as EURe to your linked wallet within ~5 seconds. Monerium covers gas costs.
- Supports 1st and 3rd party SEPA payments with no inherent transaction limits.

## Orders

[API Reference →](https://docs.monerium.com/api/#tag/orders)

### SEPA payments

**Incoming (issue orders):** Fully autonomous — Monerium creates them automatically when funds arrive. Listen for `order.created`.

**Outgoing (redeem orders):** Triggered via API. Monerium burns EURe from your wallet and sends a SEPA transfer from your IBAN. Typically processes within 5 seconds.

### Bridging / Cross-chain

Triggered by you. Initiates a redeem on the source chain and a dependent issue on the destination chain. No fees; Monerium covers gas.

### Redeem order requirements

- All redeem orders require a cryptographic signature from the linked wallet.
- Orders over **15,000 EUR** require a `supportingDocumentId`. Upload via `/files` first.

### Webhooks

- **`order.created`:** New order initialized.
- **`order.updated`:** Order state changed (e.g. `placed` → `processed`).

All payloads include a `webhook-signature` header. Verify with your shared secret.

### Endpoints

- `POST /orders` — Initiate a redeem order.
- `GET /orders` — List orders, filterable by `kind`, `state`, or `chain`.
- `GET /orders/{orderId}` — Fetch order details including `txHashes`.

## Going live

Private applications have the simplest path to production — no application review required.

1. **Signup:** Create your personal or business account at [Monerium.app](https://monerium.app).
2. **Onboarding:** Submit your KYC (individuals) or KYB (businesses) details.
3. **Profile approval:** Once approved, your Production API credentials are enabled automatically.
